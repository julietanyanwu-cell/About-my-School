About my school

The name of my school is Ennis Educate together National School.

EETNS is a public and primary school.

EETNs is a small school only consisting of 3 buildings, one for third,fourth,fifth and sixth class, another one for first, second and special class(people who are neurodivergent) and the back building is for junior and senior infants.

My school is located in Gort Road, Ennis, County Clare.

The school has 149 students enrolled.
